What we are

What Credit for Credit is

Credit for Credit is a learning platform built around credit-bearing modules. Instead of long, expensive programmes that people start and abandon, we break recognised learning into small units — days, not months — that each carry credits toward a national qualification.

The name is the model, and it works in both directions. You put in credit — time, effort, funded training — and you get credit back: recognised, banked, and portable. Academic credit becomes financial credit. The credits you earn by doing well build a record that follows you, and a record of what you can actually do is worth something to every employer who reads it.

Why it matters

Skills that count on paper, not just in practice

Plenty of people learn on the job and have nothing formal to show for it. Plenty of companies spend on training that never reaches a scorecard. Credit-bearing modules fix both ends of that: the learner banks credits toward a national qualification, and the employer's spend becomes recognised, documented B-BBEE Skills Development.
