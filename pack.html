Corporate Pack

How the pack works

Pick it, we buy it, they study

1 · They choose

An employee or executive picks a course from the pack that fits where they're going.

2 · We buy the seat

Where the course belongs to another institution, we purchase it on their behalf — one invoice to you, no procurement scramble.

3 · They study

On the provider's own platform, and they receive that provider's certificate.

4 · It lands on their record

Everything completed sits on one credit record — credits where they apply, certificates where they don't.

What counts for what

Credit-bearing modules are delivered through an accredited QCTO Skills Development Provider. They carry credits toward the Occupational Certificate: Computer Technician and the spend is recognised B-BBEE Skills Development.

Brokered courses and wellness are not. They earn the provider's own certificate, they do not carry NQF credits, and we do not claim them as scorecard spend. We'd rather tell you that now than have a verification agency tell you later.

That split is deliberate: the pack mixes what scores with what's genuinely worth doing, and labels which is which.
